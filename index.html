Po the Panda

About Po

Po is a fat panda that is somehow chosen to be the legendary dragon warrior. Motivated by food, he is trained up by master Shifu in order to take on the notorious Tai Lung. After finding that there is no secret ingredient, Po beats Tai Lung through a mixture of sheer luck and chubbiness.
